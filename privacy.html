Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the Covenant Eyes Router Project (“Project,” “we,” “our,” or “us”).

We respect your privacy and believe clarity builds trust. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your choices regarding your information when you visit our website, join the waitlist, or otherwise interact with the Covenant Eyes Router Project.

This Project is currently exploratory and pre-launch in nature. Your participation may include joining a waitlist, viewing concept materials, or submitting interest information.

Development Status

The Covenant Eyes Router Project is currently in active development and evaluation.

Features, functionality, pricing, and availability may change over time, including being added, refined, or removed, as we gather feedback and assess technical feasibility and user needs.

If you choose to join the waitlist or submit your information:

  • We may retain the information you provide for research and communication purposes.
  • The Project may evolve significantly or may not proceed to commercial launch.
  • Information submitted may still be retained even if the Project does not launch.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Email address (when joining the waitlist or expressing interest)
  • Name (if provided)
  • Package or configuration interest (e.g., 1 Pack or 2 Pack)
  • Survey responses or written feedback
  • Any additional information you submit through forms or direct communication

You are not required to provide more information than is necessary to join the waitlist.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the website, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • General geographic region (based on IP address, not precise location)

We aim to keep analytics lightweight and focused on understanding general usage patterns.

If our data practices change materially, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Share updates about the Covenant Eyes Router Project
  • Notify you of availability, beta access, or launch plans
  • Understand interest in different product configurations
  • Improve messaging, clarity, and user experience
  • Conduct internal research and product validation
  • Maintain the security and integrity of the website

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use your information for unrelated marketing outside of the Covenant Eyes ecosystem without your consent.

Legal Basis (If Applicable)

Where required by applicable law, we process personal information based on:

  • Your consent (e.g., joining the waitlist)
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., product research and improvement)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

The Covenant Eyes Router Project may rely on trusted third-party services to operate effectively. These may include:

  • Airtable (form and waitlist collection)
  • PostHog (privacy-focused analytics)
  • Email delivery services
  • Cloud hosting providers

These services operate under their own privacy policies and may process information in accordance with their terms.

We select vendors thoughtfully and aim to work with providers who align with responsible data practices.

Data Retention

We may retain your information:

  • As long as the Covenant Eyes Router Project remains active
  • As needed for internal research and product evaluation
  • For reasonable recordkeeping and compliance purposes
  • Even if the Project does not ultimately launch

You may request deletion of your information at any time (see “Your Rights” below).

Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information.

However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Maintain basic website functionality
  • Understand general usage patterns
  • Improve user experience

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the site.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for communications
  • Opt out of certain tracking technologies

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children’s Privacy

The Covenant Eyes Router Project website is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

Material changes may also be communicated through the website or via email, if appropriate.

Pilot program

This is a pilot test to gauge interest. No payment will be collected. We are validating demand for the Covenant Eyes Router.
